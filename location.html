Getting Here

Driving Directions from the Airport

If you are coming by taxi, then you just need to give him the address and the driver should be able to get you here.

If you are driving from the airport, then you follow this route:

1

Take Highway 427 South

2

Then Highway 401 West

3

Then Highway 403

4

Get off Highway 403 at the Hurontario Street exit

5

Go south on Hurontario Street till you reach Burnhamthorpe Road

6

Make a right turn into Burnhamthorpe Road (West)

7

Pass the first traffic light, at the second traffic light (Duke of York), turn left

8

3888 Duke of York will be the first building at your right
